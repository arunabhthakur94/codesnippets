Welcome to D-EDGE Career Page

Established from the merger of two long-established hospitality digital solutions providers (Availpro and Fastbooking), D-EDGE offers leading-edge cloud-based e-commerce solutions to more than 12,000 hotels in over 100 countries.

Our integrated range of solutions covers all stages of hotel distribution which encompasses Central Reservation System, Data Intelligence, Connectivity Hub, Digital Media and Website Creation.

We are a fast-growing company with a team of 350 experts located in over 20 countries, offering attractive career opportunities. D-EDGE provides localised support, services and tools. We are constantly on the lookout for new, talented individuals. You will find a list of current vacancies below. If you are interested in any of these opportunities, please submit your application.

We look forward to welcoming you on board.

Be vigilant. Fake job offers are becoming common practice. D-EDGE will never ask you to provide confidential information or bank details in a job offer.
If you receive an unexpected job offer asking you for such information, DO NOT ANSWER. In case of doubt, please contact hr@d-edge.com
