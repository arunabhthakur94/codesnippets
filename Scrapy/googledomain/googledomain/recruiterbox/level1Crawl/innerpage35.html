Welcome:

Welcome to our Careers section. Please review the positions we are currently hiring for and apply to the ones that interest you.

Our work culture:

We at Subhiksha prefer to have a familial work culture rather than a corporate setup. Subhiksha is a proven 27year old (since 1986) business house run by a small, tightly knit team of professionally educated individuals. Each and every employee in our team are bound more by personal relations than 'employee-employer contracts'.

At Subhiksha, we want to provide the best home buying experience for our customers, weather it is for a 1BHK 500 Sq.ft flat or a 2400 Sq.ft deluxe villa. As such, we expect candidates to have qualities that transalate to having a very fine attention to detail.

We consider all our employees as part of one 'Subhiksha' family and we will always consider to do so even as we grow larger and bigger.

What do we do:

We are a 29 year old property development company having developed over 120 apartments in Chennai city.

We are now expanding and are looking for enthusiastic and energetic people to be part of our growing team.

Where do we do business:

We are primarily a Chennai based real estate developer. We concentrate on a few select handpicked areas for our projects after a through market study. In the past, most of our projects have been completed in the following areas,

  • Choolaimedu - Nungambakkam - Arumbakkam area
  • Kodambakkam - Ashok Nagar - West Mambalam area
  • Vadapalani - Saligramam - Porur belt
  • Adyar - Kasturibai Nagar radius
