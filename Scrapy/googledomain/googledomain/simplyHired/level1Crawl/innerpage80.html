Senior DataOps Engineer

Crux Informatics - New York, NY

Full-time
Apply Now
Job Description

As a senior member of the DataOps team, your primary goal is to respond to production issues affecting data pipelines. You will work directly with suppliers and customers, as well as the internal engineering teams to triage and remediate issues and restore service. You should have the proven ability to access and extract data through various means, have worked on cloud hosted platforms such as GCP or AWS, and be effective in communicating with technical and non-technical teams at all levels.

Responsibilities

Provide Level 2 production support for data pipelines, working with the platform team as well as data suppliers and consumers to understand use cases and responding to any issue raised
Working in shifts you will be responsible for live monitoring of production systems and incident management of reported issues during business hours and a rotating on-call coverage
Continual Service Improvement (CSI) for the CRUX support processes through performance reporting and metrics that can help forecast and proactively address issues
About Crux

Crux helps people work in harmony with data. Our services empower companies to harness data with ease and delight. Here’s how we help our clients overcome the challenges of data:

INFORMATICS PLATFORM

We offer a secure, scalable platform where companies can store, explore, and transform data. This cloud environment helps our clients develop, implement, and manage business initiatives and workflows with much greater efficiency.

DATA CONCIERGE SERVICE

We offer a managed service for data engineering. Our team of skilled data engineers supports our clients by applying machine learning technologies to wrangle data at scale. These data concierges onboard, clean, and normalize raw data to bring our clients data that’s ready for action.

DATA NETWORK

Our network helps companies discover, evaluate, and harness high-quality data for success. We aggregate data from a wealth of sources, provide insights into vendors and datasets, and help companies gain access to data that can improve competitive advantage.

Qualifications

About You

You are naturally helpful and enjoy supporting business users and resolving technical issues
You bring experience of cloud application architecture and data life cycle
You enjoy analyzing networks, system services, operating systems and backend production systems, specifically in Python, and you are skilled at quickly identifying issues and remediating them
You love working on high-performing teams, collaborating with team members, and improving our ability to deliver delightful experiences to our clients
You are excited by the opportunity to solve challenging technical problems, and you find learning about data fascinating
You understand Server, Network, and Hosting Environments, RESTful and other common APIs, common data distribution, and hosted storage solutions
Must Have

10+ years of experience in a support or operations role, preferable in a technology or financial services company supporting production environments with enterprise users
ITSM literate with strong documentation instincts
Experience working in a cloud-based environment, such as GCP or the AWS
Proficiency with data pipelines and ETL
Working knowledge of OO Python and SQL
Experience working as part of a cross function matrixed support team
Strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills
Ability to exercise judgment to make sound decisions
Strong communications skills, interpersonal skills, and a sense of humor
Bachelor's degree in an engineering discipline or equivalent experience
Even Better

Data skills: SQL and NOSQL, structured and unstructured data, BigQuery
Experience in Airflow, GitHub and working with ETL tools
Big data and cloud processing experience, e.g. HDFS, Spark
Experience working with market data
