Connect all the dots on one powerful platform
Employee Information
  • Securely store sensitive information for all your employees in one place.
  • Quickly find information like Start Date, Salary, Address, Social Security Number, Emergency Contacts and more.
Time Off Tracking
  • Effortlessly keep track of all time off requests and records for all of your employees.
  • Create custom time off policies and keep track of time off history/records for their employees.
  • Employees can request time off by sending a request directly to their manager for review. Both employee and manager can check for time off conflicts with other team members. The feature automatically updates time off balances, making the process simple for both employee and manager.
Document Management
  • Send documents to employees to be electronically signed and store them on our HRIS.
  • Upload documents, create signature templates and send those to employees. Employees can electronically sign the documents.
  • Securely store and organize documents , saving you time and a headache from trying to locate files in a cabinet.
Organizational Chart
  • Display your workforce on a professional organizational chart while giving the ability for employees to easily find and contact fellow team members.
  • Your organizational chart is automatically created when employees are added to the SimplyHired HRIS, making it easy for all users to view the different teams and roles within your company.
