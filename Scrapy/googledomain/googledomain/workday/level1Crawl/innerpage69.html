Hospitality.

Aligning talent with financial and organizational priorities is now easier than ever. Workday brings finance and HR into a single system, providing you with actionable people and financial data. You can efficiently manage and develop your workforce and respond faster to change, freeing up time for guest services.

See Workday applications for hospitality.

Many of the world’s leading hospitality companies use Workday.

Manage and engage your entire workforce.

Understand the cost and capabilities of your entire workforce—including hourly, seasonal, and contingent workers; store/property managers; and staff at the corporate office.

With Workday, managers can access financial and worker information at any location—on any device—to make decisions faster. Restaurant and property managers can rapidly recruit, hire, and onboard employees. And because Workday is in the cloud, IT can shift from managing infrastructure to delivering on more strategic priorities, such as loyalty programs and customer experience.

With Workday you can:

  • Respond quickly to business, organizational, and regulatory changes
  • Reduce turnover by cultivating a culture of career development and opportunity
  • Gain operational efficiencies to free up time for enhanced guest services
  • Make strategic and operational decisions with real-time business analytics

Applications for the hospitality industry.

Easily onboard new employees, manage a dynamic workforce, and get a full view into operations and business performance across locations—all using one seamless finance and HR system.
