8 West Consulting is an enterprise software development company located in Cork, specialising in architecting, developing and integrating custom .Net and Java applications. With over 170 software technology professionals on staff, 8 West Consulting provides unique, customer-specific, cost-efficient solutions for clients including eBay Enterprise, Avon, Bath and Body Works, Expedia and a number of large US healthcare insurance carriers. Serving both private and public sector organisations, 8 West focuses on Architecture Blueprinting, Bespoke Application Development, eCommerce, Product Development and Digital Innovation.

8 West Consulting was established in 1998 originally as a subsidiary of DeCare International and was subsequently acquired by Anthem LLC. 8 West was originally created to support the information technology needs of Anthem and its companies. 8 West is now an independently owned Irish consulting company.

Our philosophy at 8 West Consulting is to provide our employees with an engaging, rich, creative and collaborative environment, to further their skills, interests and experience in their particular field, and indeed other fields in which they display interest and aptitude.

We pride ourselves first and foremost on being a 'Technologist's' company.
