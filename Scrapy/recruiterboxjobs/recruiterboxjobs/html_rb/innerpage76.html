About Us

The three founders of LNG Publishing Co., Lisa Tocci, Nancy DeMarco, and Gloria Steinberg Briskin worked together for an oil industry trade association. After a successful career in trade association management, we founded our company and launched our flagship publication, Lubes'n'Greases in 1995. The print edition is free to qualified subscribers in the United States and Canada and available on a paid-subscription basis elsewhere. The digital edition, an exact replica of the print magazine, is free to qualified subscribers worldwide. Since then, we have added several other traditional media and data products -- all aimed at the global lubricants industry.

Our publications are controlled circulation and are produced by our talented staff of writers, editors, and designers. Many of these individuals have been with us for many years, and that stability is one of the hallmarks of our success. Most of us are based at the company's headquarters in Falls Church, Virginia; however, we do have some staff working remotely.

As we approach the end of our second decade in business, we look forward to expanding our digital presence with social media, increasing our influence in the Asia/Pacific region, and formalizing many of the processes that we have developed and refined over the years.


Our Team

Our staff is distinguished by its ntegrity and commitment to quality. We adhere to the highest journalistic standards, maintaining a high wall between our editorial and advertising operations.

To be successful at LNG Publishing Co., employees must be strong collaborators, respecting the opinions of others, but unafraid of rocking the boat in the presentation of their ideas -- especially in matters where the reputation of our firm or publications is at stake. Because publishing is an extremely competitive business, we appreciate individuals who approach their work strategically and in an organized fashion. A commitment to timely but thoughtful execution of one's responsibilities is necessary and a ready sense of humor is an asset in our deadline-driven environment.

At its heart, LNG Publishing Co. is a firm with a solid, well-respected foundation. As the firm grows and expands its products and services, there will be opportunites for new and additional responsibilities for our staff. If you are looking for a stable employer who can provide you with an opportunity to do good work and the freedom to do it, we might be the right place for you to continue your successful career trajectory.
