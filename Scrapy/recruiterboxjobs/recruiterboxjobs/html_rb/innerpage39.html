Eurojob offers you a lot!

With an impressive labour force Eurojob - www.eurojob.nl - is one of the leading international employment agencies in the Netherlands since the year 2000.

Eurojob services well-known clients in retail, logistics & transport, production & assembly, air cargo handling, metal & engineering and food processing.

Eurojob is constantly offering qualified men and women opportunities to making progress in their working careers, so they can seriously have a great future in the Netherlands.

Eurojob takes good care of its employees by offering housing close to their work address, transport, insurances and training opportunities. And of course Eurojob pays you well.

When you think you have the right quality, education and mentality to be part of Eurojob's Topteams for Topjobs , then respond as soon as possible.
