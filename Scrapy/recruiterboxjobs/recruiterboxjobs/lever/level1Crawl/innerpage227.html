Meet Lever’s Sales Leadership Team

Lever
Follow
Apr 18, 2018 · 5 min read

Our Sales team is growing — fast! We’ve welcomed over 50 new team members and six new key leaders to Lever in the past year, and we’re ready to tackle our biggest year yet.

We are still looking for new team members to join us. Meet the folks at the helm below, hear from some members of the Sales team , and if your interest is piqued, get in touch .

Rob Tomchick, SVP Sales

What were you doing before Lever?

I was with SAP for 7 years (through their acquisition of Business Objects) before joining NetSuite to lead their Software & Services vertical as well as their Private Equity & VC team for 5 years prior to their acquisition by Oracle. Most recently, I led sales at Percolate before joining Lever.

What is Lever poised to do in the next couple of years? Why is this an exciting time to be here?

Lever has product market fit in a massive market, and we have the opportunity and challenge to execute on it and prove that we’re the dominant player in this industry over the next two years. Since joining last year my main focus has been growing our sales team, with an extra focus on leadership, development and world-class operations. We’re still building our team, but have the core leadership in place to step into the vacant role as the default product in the talent landscape. There is so much opportunity for growth, creativity and for someone to come in and help us shake up the industry. We’re at an exciting inflection point for Lever.

Tammy Aguillon, Senior Enterprise Sales Director

What were you doing before Lever?

I was a sales leader at a few high-growth startups (Responsys, Xamarin, DoubleDutch), then subsequently at the companies that acquired them (Oracle and Microsoft).

What makes you excited about the opportunity at Lever?

There’s so much about Lever that excites me. Our product is incredible — we hear from customers every day about how much transformation they’re able to drive with our Lever. Next is our market opportunity — there’s so much untapped potential in the enterprise segment for a product like Lever and the market has been under-served for so long by Incumbents. Last but certainly not least, it’s the people I get to work with. We have a dedicated and passionate sales team that’s continuously working on stretching ourselves beyond just hitting a number.

Jessica Green, Senior Director of Mid Market Sales

What were you doing before Lever?

I started my career in the ATS space, serving staffing and recruitment firms and then transitioned to ToutApp where I lead sales and provided a platform that powered the productivity of sales organizations. I love being part of companies that creates personalized software that makes people work lives (and in turn their personal lives) even better!

What attracted you to Lever?

Where do I start?! People. Market Opportunity. Product. Personal and Professional Growth. I spent the last 4 years partnering with sales organizations, so I have see a TON. Lever’s sales culture and overall the people I have met are truly special. They are empathetic, authentic and passionate about their peers, their customers and the impact they make. The next three really go together. The addressable market for Lever is massive and it is filled with companies that are looking for ways to elevate the recruitment process and their get credit for the value of their organization. We have the product that changes the game for recruitment teams and the companies they are building. I am ecstatic to be on this ride with Lever!

Hear more from Jess.

Arie Jongejan, Director of Enterprise Sales

What were you doing before Lever?

Before joining Lever I was working in Sales and Sales Management at Adobe, which I joined via the acquisition of Livefyre, a B2B SaaS startup.

Why is now the right time to join the team?

I’m obviously a bit biased, but I genuinely believe now is the perfect time to join the team at Lever. We have a massive total addressable market paired with the best product in the mid-market, which is fun, strategic & fascinating to sell and that customers truly love. The entire leadership team, myself included, is fully committed to your success in your role and your personal and professional development.

And of course, the people and the environment. Peers that will make you want to elevate your game across the board and a workplace that people love, where we practice what we preach on empathy and inclusion.

Hear more from Arie.

Lindsey Swanson, Director, Customer Accounts Executives

What were you doing before Lever?

I started my career in international development, and spent a cumulative two years in India, Nepal, and Southeast Asia doing microfinance and female empowerment work. Back in the US, I moved into sales at a consulting and advisory firm, before falling in love with tech over 5 years ago. Before Lever, I led a customer-growth department for a manager-development software company. I loved getting my management start at a company that studied effective management.

Is there anything you think someone should know about working at Lever?

Lever has a rare amalgamation of very special people. The team is talented, bright, driven, thoughtful, and kind. There’s a concentration of inspiring people. I think that helps Lever bring out the best in its people, and I see people reach new heights every day.
