About This Site

Lever eliminates the burden of manual work in the hiring process, freeing up teams to get back to the real work of recruiting. With Lever, fragmented communication and data are a thing of the past. Recruiters, hiring managers, interviewers, and leadership have a source of truth for all hiring activity. And with Lever's real-time reports, teams can easily target where to double-down on hiring efforts.
