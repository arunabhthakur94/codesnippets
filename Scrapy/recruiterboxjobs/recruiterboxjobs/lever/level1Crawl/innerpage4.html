Lever Brand Guidelines


Overview

To preserve the integrity of our identity, we want to ensure that both the Lever logo and mark are always recognizable and legible, and stand out against any competing design elements.

Logo

This is the official Lever logo and mark for our company brand. For most cases, use the standard logo for white or light solid background colors. Inverted versions are also available for dark or other solid background colors. Most importantly, refer to our safe space and minimum size guidelines to ensure that the logo is legible with enough color contrast for accessibility.

Guidelines

There should always be safe space free of any graphics or text surrounding the Lever logo. The ideal safe space is found by taking the height distance (x) of the logo and applying the same distance from the outer edges of the Lever logo or mark. Make sure that logo is not smaller than the defined minimum sizes for any format including web, print, and promotional items.

Safe space

Minimum size (digital)

To ensure the logo is always readable digitally, the logo height should always be 24px or larger.

Minimum size - Print

The minimum logo height for print is 0.25 inches or 7 mm.

Partner logos

Logo misuse

The Lever logo is never meant to be altered in anyway. Please treat our logo with respect and avoid the following scenarios, some of which are illustrated below.

Don’t change the color of any part of the logo
Don’t distort the shape or proportion of the logo
Don’t rotate or turn the logo from the rest of the content
Don’t use the Lever word mark as a standalone without the logo mark
Never compromise or manipulate the Lever identity in anyway
